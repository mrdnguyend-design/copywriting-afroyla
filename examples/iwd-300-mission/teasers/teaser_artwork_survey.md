# TEASER | 230226 | Artwork Survey — "The Lady Looks Like Me"

**Subject:** The lady on the bag looks like me.

**Preview:** She said it with a crown on her head.

---

*"The lady on the bag looks like me with a crown. It is absolutely stunning."*

I read that three times. Put my phone down. Just... sat there.

It was Tuesday night. Couch. Tea going cold. I was reading reviews — not the five-star "great bag!" kind. Those are nice. But every now and then someone writes something that stops you cold.

This was one of those.

A woman who bought our Aina Lime Pop.

Not: "the artwork is pretty."

Not: "I like the design."

*The lady on the bag looks like ME.*

I forwarded the review to Keisha at 11:47 PM.

One line: *"Read this."*

(I should probably stop texting Keisha at midnight. But she texted back in 90 seconds, so... she was up too.)

---

By Thursday, she walked into my office with her sketchbook.

Not walked. *Charged.*

"Michelle. SIT DOWN."

(I was already sitting.)

"Look."

She flipped the sketchbook open.

6 new artwork designs for the Medium bag.

One had dot-work so fine it looked like the woman was made of stars.

Another was pop-art — "BLACK IS BEAUTIFUL" woven right into the design.

Another was all black with white dot-work. Elegant. Quiet. The kind of quiet that makes the whole room lean in.

That one scared me a little. The good kind of scared.

6 designs. 6 different women staring back at you.

I'm not describing the other three. Go see them yourself.

I looked up at her.

"Keisha. We can't put ALL of these into production."

She closed the sketchbook. Looked at me.

"I know."

Pause.

"That's why I need THEM to decide."

---

So here we are.

6 artwork designs. We can't make all of them.

**You're going to decide which ones we actually produce.**

Same process as always: you tell us what you want, we build it.

Takes about 3 minutes.

The ones YOU love the most? Those are the ones going on the bag.
