### IDEA A: "I Didn't Get It From George" — Full George Origin Story ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Scene concept:**
George has been unbearable this week. Conference tournaments started and this man has brackets EVERYWHERE. Printed brackets on the kitchen counter. Bracket on his phone. Bracket breakdown at the dinner table. "Michelle, Duke is a lock for the one seed." "Michelle, you gotta watch the conference finals tonight." "Michelle, Selection Sunday is in THREE days."

She's not listening. She's thinking about the sale. About what to call three days of madness where everything goes for $9.95.

And then George says the word.

"This is the best part of March. It's just pure madness."

And Michelle freezes.

"...what did you just say?"

George looks up. "What? March Madness? It's MARCH MADNESS week, Michelle, everybody knows that—"

"George."

"What."

"Nothing."

She walks to her laptop. Opens the campaign file. Types: MARCH MADNESS.

George appears in the doorway. "Did you just... did you take that from me?"

"I don't know what you're talking about."

"MICHELLE. I SAW YOU TYPE IT."

"George, the NCAA does not own the word 'madness.'"

"You got the name from me."

"I got the name from the SITUATION, George. Which is that I'm selling $140 bags for $14.95, which is MADNESS, which has nothing to do with your brackets."

Beat.

"...but also yes. Don't tell anyone."
