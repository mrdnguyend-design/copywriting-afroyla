## 10. LAST MONTH'S LESSONS

> From `learnings/monthly-insights.md` + March performance

- **What worked:** IWD 300 Mission — George character arc carried emotional weight across entire campaign. Customer co-creation (voting on bags) drove unprecedented engagement.
- **What to avoid:** SMS ≠ email voice. Don't script comedy in SMS — let situations be funny naturally.
- **New style rules added:** #17 (em dash overuse), #24 (no triple-And anaphora), #25 (polite vs quiet reinterpretation)
- **Best performing theme:** Community Win (IWD 300 Mission)
- **Best performing character:** George (comedy → attendance → silence → DONE arc)
- **RPS changes:** Migrated from Klaviyo-reported to GA4 attribution — E_Sale jumped from $0.012 to $0.167
