- **Name:** Early Mother's Day Sale 2026
- **Positioning:** "Invitation to a dinner party, not a doorbuster" — list-only, luxury-heritage feel, anti-QVC
- **Why this campaign, why now:** Largest revenue event of 2026 H1 (50% of monthly revenue weight). Heritage anchor (Mother's Day May 10). Capitalize on registered queens segment grown from 1.5K → 18K via Apr 10-20 FB Ads push.
- **Target segment(s):** REG primary (gate), MASS for registration push only, HIGH for daily story bridges
