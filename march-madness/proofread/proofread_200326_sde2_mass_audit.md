- ✅ **Stunted Pacing:** Not present. Fast launch email — appropriate.
- ✅ **Lead Failure:** PASS. "I just hit publish" = relevant, brief, human.
- ✅ **Muddled Argument:** Not present. Linear: publish → price shock → others are shopping → text AFR995E.
- ✅ **No Sales Desire:** FIXED. Price shock ("$39, $42, $45. Today? $9.95") builds desire.

- ⚠️ **Logic Flaw:** "They got their link at 10AM sharp" — but this email sends at 10:30 AM. Mass reads this and thinks: "Wait, OTHER people got links 30 min before me?" That's actually GOOD FOMO. **Before:** "Just the page, the prices, and 88 designs that won't all be there tomorrow."

Everything else = clean. No em dashes. No AI-isms. Michelle's voice throughout.

## 3. Rationale

- "Won't all be there tomorrow" implies everything disappears overnight. "The best ones won't last" = truthful scarcity without overpromising.
