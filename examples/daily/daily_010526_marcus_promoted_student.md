# DAILY | 010526 | Marcus_Promoted_Student

**Subject:** I just got promoted to student.
**Preview:** Marcus walked me to the whiteboard. Said five words. Picked up the marker.

---

Marcus has worked here three years. Never once told me how to do my job.

This morning he did.

He stood in my doorway before I'd finished my coffee and said *About that May 1 thing.*

I followed him to the whiteboard. I think I just got promoted to student.

He doesn't usually do this. Marcus packs boxes. Texts me when shipments land. Stares at clipboards.

Today he picked up the marker. Drew three boxes.

*Economy.* He wrote a date next to it. **May 9 to 12.**

*Standard.* **May 6 to 9.**

*Express.* **May 4 to 5.**

Then he turned around and looked at me.

*You've been pointing at Economy like it's the safe one. May 11 is in this window. So is May 12.*

I sat down.

*(I don't usually sit down mid-conversation.)*

He kept going. Quiet, the way Marcus is quiet. Not mad. Just done watching me write the wrong thing.

*Standard lands by Saturday. May 9. That's the lane I'd pick. Costs a few dollars more. No panic.*

*Express?* He tapped the third box. *May 4 to 5. Next weekend.*

Then it hit me. Express means she has it before Mother's Day even shows up. The surprise twice. Once when she opens it. Once Sunday morning when she carries it to church.

I looked at the whiteboard. Then I looked at Marcus.

*So I've been scaring people who didn't need scaring.*

*A little, yeah.*

He went back to the warehouse.

So here's what Marcus says you should know.

There isn't one deadline. There's three lanes.

Economy fits your wallet? Go ahead. Just know May 11 is on the table. If she's the kind of mother who opens it on the day, the day might be Tuesday.

Want her to hold it on May 10? Pick **Standard.** That's the floor. Lands by Saturday. Marcus's pick.

Or if you want the surprise twice — Express. May 4 to 5. Walk it in next Saturday. Watch her face once. Then again Sunday morning at church.

Three lanes. One mother. Pick the risk you can live with.

Code **MD2026** still takes 60% off. That part hasn't changed. Just the part where I had it wrong about Economy.
