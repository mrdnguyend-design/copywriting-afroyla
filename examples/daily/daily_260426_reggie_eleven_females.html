There's a type of email I wait for.

It usually comes from a man. A husband. A son. A brother. Sometimes a grandfather.

He doesn't write to tell me he liked the bag.

He writes to tell me about the woman he gave it to. What her face did when she opened the box. Whether she covered her mouth. The thing she said before she said anything else. How long it took her to talk.

I love these emails the most.

One came in yesterday. From a man named Reggie.

Here's what he wrote:

"The purse and handbag that I have received so far were in perfect condition and made very nice gifts. I was extremely proud when I saw my wife's face as it was a surprise."

"Signed by a grandfather with 11 females in my family i love the mostest."

I read it. Then I read it again. Then I put my phone down for a minute.

Eleven women. One grandfather. He didn't list their names. Didn't have to. He just said the number, then "the mostest."

Eleven daughters? Granddaughters? A wife? Sisters? I don't know. Reggie didn't say. What I do know is that he's working through them, one bag at a time.

His wife already has the Kadia. Top Grain Leather. That's the one he was talking about. The one he watched her open. The next one (the Eliora, also Top Grain) is on the way. He didn't tell me who it's for. Maybe he hasn't decided yet. Maybe he has and he's keeping it quiet.

I checked the calendar after I read his email. Then I checked it again.

Reggie ordered both bags before today. That's a good thing. Because today is April 26. Mother's Day is May 10. That's two weeks.

The Early Mother's Day Sale closed Thursday. But the wider Mother's Day pricing is still up. Up to 60% off. All the way through May 10. The catch is the calendar, not the price.

If you want the bag in her hands BEFORE Mother's Day morning, the safe order date is May 1.

That's not a deadline I'm making up. That's the deadline the calendar made up. Eight or nine days for shipping, plus a small buffer, lands you safely on May 10. After May 1 we'll still ship. We just can't promise the morning.

So if you've been thinking about it (your mom, your wife, your sister, your daughter, your best friend), Reggie is two bags ahead of you. You've got five days.
