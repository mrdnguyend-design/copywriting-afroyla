And since you're here, I thought I'd share a little backstory.

I've always had a passion for fashion and design. I even have a degree in retail with a concentration in fashion merchandising.

But for years, the dream of creating my own brand stayed quietly tucked away.

Life had a way of pulling me in different directions. Until finally, in 2024, I decided to listen to that dream and start Afroyla (we were called Melanista back then).

We began small. Crafting made-to-order handbags inspired by the pride, strength, and beauty of Black women.

Every piece made with love and intention.

But being a tiny brand just starting out, made-to-order was all we could afford.

The tradeoff?

Longer shipping times.

And I knew that wasn't the experience I wanted for the women we serve.

So in 2025, we made a bold shift.

We moved to ready-to-ship inventory based in the U.S. Rebranded as Afroyla. And committed to making luxury-inspired handbags accessible and fast.

Because you deserve better. Better designs, better service, better experiences.
