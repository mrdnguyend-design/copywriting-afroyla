## Notes

- **Different from BLS pattern.** BLS SMS = "I got a text from [person]" → line by line reaction. This = Michelle NARRATING what Katie did. "This woman just sent me her GAME PLAN." She's telling the story, not forwarding the texts. The structure is a mini-scene, not a forwarded message.
- **"You know Katie from the nail salon right?"** — Conversational. Like talking to a friend. "You know that girl? Well guess what she did." Different from "I just got a text from Katie" (which is relay format).
- **Katie's game plan as a LIST Michelle received.** "With BULLET POINTS." — The comedy is that Katie treated this sale like a work project. She submitted a plan. With formatting.
- **"That's not a lot, that's a STEAL."** — Katie reframes. Not "I'm insane." Instead: "the prices are insane, I'm just being smart." Reader agrees.
- **"The only insane thing is NOT buying 12."** — Flip. Now NOT shopping is the crazy behavior. Reader's perspective shifts.
- **"Are you??"** — Two question marks. Direct peer pressure through a question. Not "register now."
- **Katie = nail salon callback.** Connects SM1 storyline. Recurring character building.
