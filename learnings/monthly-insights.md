## Campaign Deep-Dives

### March 2026 — IWD 300 Mission Campaign

**Result:** 300 orders / 3 days. $2,500 → BWHI CYL2. Campaign complete on IWD.

**Content volume:** 28 pieces (5 teasers + 19 sale-day + 2 brainstorms + 1 recap + 1 confirmation).

**What scaled:** George. Comedy → attendance → silence → DONE = full character arc. By D3, readers watched for him. His silence at 275 = strongest moment — comedy going quiet = something is real.

**What differentiated:** Competitive context opening (23 IWD emails, none did anything). Graded A+ by Ben/Daniel. Best cold-reader opening in the series.

**What held together:** $5 mechanic. Simple, repeatable, equalizing. "Same $5. Same seat at the table."

**Biggest lesson:** SMS ≠ email. SMS = short, ridiculous, group-chat energy. Email = scene-based, earned weight. Never mix the voices.
