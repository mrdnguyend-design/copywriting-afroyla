# 📱 VIP FLASH DEAL SMS — Template System

> **Segment:** BLS-Registered (high-engaged customers)
> **Frequency:** 8-10x/month (2-3x/week)
> **Duration:** 24 hours only per deal
> **Voice:** Michelle texting her close friend, NOT a brand announcement
> **Goal:** Entertaining + exclusive feel. They should LOOK FORWARD to these texts.

---

## 🎯 GOLDEN RULES

1. **Never say "VIP" or "EXCLUSIVE"** — show it through behavior, not labels
2. **One joke/angle per SMS** — don't stack comedy. One killer line > three ok ones
3. **Lead with the relationship**, not the deal — "Sis, I did something" > "FLASH SALE 🚨"
4. **Keep under 320 chars** in the core message (before link) — Klaviyo SMS sweet spot
5. **Rotate angles** — never use the same angle two sends in a row
6. **Product name + price anchor always included** — "[Product]" $X.XX (normally $XX.XX)
7. **One character max per SMS** — George OR Alice OR no one. Never both.
8. **End with warmth** — "— Michelle 💜" or "— Michelle" (never "— Afroyla Team")

---

## 🔄 10 ROTATING ANGLES

Use these angles in rotation. Each has a different comedy/emotional flavor so 8-10 sends/month feel fresh.

---

### ANGLE 1: "I Saved You One" (Warmth + Loyalty)
**Vibe:** Michelle personally set something aside for you because you've been there since day one.

**Template:**
```
{{ first_name|default:'Sis' }}. Don't tell anyone I sent you this.

"[PRODUCT NAME]." $[DEAL PRICE]. Normally $[NORMAL PRICE].

I haven't put this anywhere yet. No email blast, no IG, nothing. Just you.

I set one aside because you've been here since day one and I don't forget that.

👉 [LINK]

Go before I change my mind. Or before Alice sees the price. Whichever comes first. 😭

— Michelle
```

**Best for:** Premium products, wallets, statement bags

---

### ANGLE 2: "I Woke Up Petty" (Ridiculous Reasoning)
**Vibe:** Michelle did something stupid-generous for a hilariously petty reason.

**Template:**
```
{{ first_name|default:'Hey love' }}. It's [TIME]. I know.

But I'm sitting here looking at these "[PRODUCT NAME]" and I decided to do something ridiculous.

$[DEAL PRICE]. Yes the $[NORMAL PRICE] ones. [MATERIAL]. I KNOW.

This isn't going to the full list. This is just for my girls who actually open my texts and show up every time.

That's you. So here: 👉 [LINK]

[RIDICULOUS CLOSER]. — Michelle 💜
```

**Ridiculous closer options (rotate):**
- "When these sell out I'm going back to bed."
- "I'm blaming this on my second cup of coffee."
- "If anyone asks, this never happened."
- "I'm going to pretend I didn't do this."

**Best for:** Any product, especially when deal is absurdly deep

---

### ANGLE 3: "George Doesn't Know" (Conspiracy Comedy)
**Vibe:** Michelle and the reader are in a secret conspiracy. George/Alice are the outsiders.

**Template:**
```
{{ first_name|default:'Sis' }}. Quick. Between us.

"[PRODUCT NAME]." $[DEAL PRICE]. Was $[NORMAL PRICE].

George doesn't know. Alice DEFINITELY doesn't know. The only people who know are you, me, and God. And God is judging me right now.

👉 [LINK]

Grab yours and we never speak of this price again. 🤫

— Michelle
```

**Variations:**
- "Alice is in a meeting. I have approximately 47 minutes of unsupervised access to the pricing dashboard."
- "George is at the grocery store. I have a 20-minute window. MOVE."
- "Alice went to lunch. I did something. She's going to need TWO lunches when she gets back."

**Best for:** When the deal is SO deep it feels "wrong." Products $10 and under.

---

### ANGLE 4: "My Thank You" (Pure Gratitude)
**Vibe:** Simple, direct, emotional. No comedy. Just Michelle thanking her day-ones.

**Template:**
```
{{ first_name|default:'Love' }}. This is just a thank you.

You open my emails. You click my links. You text back. You SHOW UP.

So today, "[PRODUCT NAME]" is $[DEAL PRICE] for you. Just you. Normally $[NORMAL PRICE].

No big announcement. No countdown. Just me saying I see you and I appreciate you.

👉 [LINK]

24 hours. Then it's gone.

With love,
Michelle 💜
```

**Best for:** Use sparingly — 1x every 2 weeks max. Best after a big sale or busy period. Emotional reset.

---

### ANGLE 5: "The Dare" (Competitive/Playful)
**Vibe:** Michelle dares the reader to act fast. Playful challenge energy.

**Template:**
```
{{ first_name|default:'Sis' }}. I have a dare for you.

"[PRODUCT NAME]." I put it at $[DEAL PRICE]. Normally $[NORMAL PRICE].

The dare: grab it before [NUMBER] other queens on this text list do. Because they got the same message. And they are FAST.

You've got 24 hours. But honestly? Last time I did this, [PRODUCT] was gone in [TIME].

Your move: 👉 [LINK]

— Michelle 😏
```

**Best for:** Products with limited stock. Creates natural urgency without fake scarcity.

---

### ANGLE 6: "George's Review" (Comedy Gold)
**Vibe:** George said/did something stupid about the product. Michelle uses it to sell.

**Template:**
```
{{ first_name|default:'Girl' }}. George just held up the "[PRODUCT NAME]" and said "[GEORGE QUOTE]."

[MICHELLE'S DEADPAN RESPONSE]

Anyway. $[DEAL PRICE]. Normally $[NORMAL PRICE]. 24 hours. Just for you.

👉 [LINK]

— Michelle (currently ignoring George) 😂
```

**George quote bank (customize per product):**
- Wallet: "Can I keep my fishing license in it?" → "George. Go away."
- Tote: "Will it fit my bowling ball?" → "George. That's not— just GO."
- Handbag: "I could carry MY stuff in this." → "You carry a wallet and a phone, George. You don't NEED a handbag."
- Round bag: "It's shaped like a CIRCLE." → "...yes, George. Thank you for that observation."
- Mini satchel: "That's too small for my lunch." → "It's not FOR your lunch, George."

**Best for:** When you need pure comedy. Lighter products, fun colors.

---

### ANGLE 7: "Alice Will Find Out" (Ticking Clock Comedy)
**Vibe:** Alice is going to discover the price and lose it. Real urgency = when Alice finds out.

**Template:**
```
{{ first_name|default:'Sis' }}. I need you to move fast. Not because of a timer — because of Alice.

"[PRODUCT NAME]." $[DEAL PRICE]. Normally $[NORMAL PRICE].

Alice doesn't check the pricing sheet until [TIME/DAY]. When she does, this price DIES and I have to sit through a 45-minute spreadsheet lecture.

Help me make it worth the lecture: 👉 [LINK]

24 hours. Or whenever Alice gets to row 47. Whichever comes first. 😭

— Michelle
```

**Best for:** Mid-range products where the discount is significant

---

### ANGLE 8: "The Confessional" (Vulnerable + Funny)
**Vibe:** Michelle confesses she's done something financially irresponsible. Reads like a group chat confession.

**Template:**
```
{{ first_name|default:'Sis' }}. I need to confess something.

I priced the "[PRODUCT NAME]" at $[DEAL PRICE].

It's normally $[NORMAL PRICE]. It's [MATERIAL/QUALITY DETAIL]. And I put it at [DEAL PRICE]. On purpose. With my own two hands.

[CONFESSION LINE]

Yours if you want it: 👉 [LINK]

24 hours then I come to my senses. — Michelle
```

**Confession line options:**
- "I am not okay and I don't want to talk about it."
- "My accountant is going to put me in financial time-out."
- "I can't explain my reasoning because there IS no reasoning."
- "If this were a crime, the motive would be 'felt cute, might delete later.'"
- "I blame the full moon."

**Best for:** When the deal is genuinely crazy. Wallets, accessories, clearance items.

---

### ANGLE 9: "The Receipt" (Ultra-Short, Punchy)
**Vibe:** Minimal words. Maximum impact. Like a friend forwarding you a secret link with zero context.

**Template:**
```
{{ first_name|default:'Sis' }}.

"[PRODUCT NAME]"
Was: $[NORMAL PRICE]
Now: $[DEAL PRICE]
Who: just you
How long: 24 hours

👉 [LINK]

[ONE-LINER]

— M 💜
```

**One-liner options:**
- "Don't make me beg."
- "You're welcome in advance."
- "I already know you're clicking."
- "Tell nobody."
- "This is the text."

**Best for:** When you've sent 2-3 longer SMS that week and need a palate cleanser. Also good for repeat products.

---

### ANGLE 10: "The Story Text" (Mini Narrative)
**Vibe:** Michelle texts about something that JUST happened. The deal comes from a real moment.

**Template:**
```
{{ first_name|default:'Sis' }}. [JUST HAPPENED MOMENT].

And I looked at the "[PRODUCT NAME]" and thought of you.

$[DEAL PRICE]. Was $[NORMAL PRICE]. For my girls only. 24 hours.

👉 [LINK]

[WARM CLOSER]

— Michelle 💜
```

**"Just happened" moment bank:**
- "I was packing orders this morning and a queen from [CITY] bought 3 of these. THREE."
- "Somebody left a review last night that made me tear up. She said [SHORT QUOTE]."
- "Tasha just showed me a DM from a sister who styled the [PRODUCT] with [OUTFIT] and it looked INSANE."
- "Marcus almost smiled when he saw how many of these are left. ALMOST. (You know Marcus.)"
- "I was on the phone with my mama and she said 'you should bless your best customers today.' So here I am. Blessed and obedient."

**Best for:** When there IS a real story or customer moment to reference. Most authentic angle.

## 📅 MONTHLY ROTATION CALENDAR

For 10 sends/month, rotate like this to avoid repetition:

| Week | Send 1 (Mon/Tue) | Send 2 (Wed/Thu) | Send 3 (Fri/Sat) |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| **Week 1** | Angle 1 (Saved You One) | Angle 6 (George's Review) | Angle 3 (George Doesn't Know) |
| **Week 2** | Angle 8 (Confessional) | Angle 9 (The Receipt) | Angle 5 (The Dare) |
| **Week 3** | Angle 10 (Story Text) | Angle 7 (Alice Will Find Out) | Angle 2 (Woke Up Petty) |
| **Week 4** | Angle 4 (My Thank You) | — | — |

### Rotation rules:
- **Never use the same angle twice in one week**
- **Angle 4 (My Thank You) max 1x every 2 weeks** — overuse kills sincerity
- **Angle 9 (The Receipt) after a busy week** — brevity = palate cleanser
- **George angles (3, 6) at least 1x/week** — audience expects and loves George
- **Angle 10 (Story Text) only when you have a real story** — don't fake it

---

## 🎭 PRODUCT-SPECIFIC NOTES

| Product Type | Best Angles | Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| **Wallets** | 1, 3, 8 (price shock works best) | 5 (hard to "dare" on a wallet) |
| **Handbags** | 5, 6, 10 (story + competition) | 9 (need more words for bags) |
| **Tote Bags** | 2, 7, 10 (everyday product = everyday story) | 4 (too emotional for tote) |
| **Mini Satchels** | 6, 8, 9 (fun + punchy) | 4 (save gratitude for bigger items) |
| **Round Bags** | 3, 5, 6 (bold product = bold angle) | 7 (Alice angle better for $$ items) |
