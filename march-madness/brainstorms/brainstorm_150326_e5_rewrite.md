### IDEA 1: "He Shipped Out Eight Bags" ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — TOP PICK

**Law:** #17 (Interesting Facts) + #1 (Personality — Michelle's reaction)
**Angle:** The MLK husband's review + photo as the centerpiece. A man who bought during the last sale — not one bag, EIGHT — for every woman in his life. His wife was already a walking billboard. Now he's shipping the rest of them out. That photo of the desk is the image.

**Opening:**
> A man sent me a photo this week.
>
> Eight bags. Lined up on a desk. Wrapped in tissue paper. Tags still on. Ready to ship.
>
> He didn't work in a warehouse. He was sitting at his kitchen table. And every single one of those bags was going to a different woman in his life.

**Flow:**

- Michelle received this review from the MLK sale. She reads his words aloud (raw, unedited):
  - His wife has the Huriya — "she takes it everywhere, always shows it off, always tells people where we got it from"
  - "When the big MLK sale happened, we jumped on board and got some items for those special ladies in our lives"
  - "Now I am getting ready to ship them all out"
- Michelle's reaction: "Eight bags. His wife already had hers. And when the MLK sale came around at 60% off, he bought one for every woman he loved."
- **THE TURN:** "That sale was in January. March 20th, it happens again. Same prices. Same leather. Same tissue paper. $9.95 wallets. $14.95 bags."
- Brief cascade: He's not the only one. TyR Den bought matching Imani bags for her AND her daughter. Jessica bought for her bestie — "she started to cry." Vincent bought for his wife, mother, sister, and mother-in-law. Mrs. YAR bought 3 extra wallets just for gifts.
- "When the prices are this low, people don't buy one. They buy one for everyone they love."
- Sale info → CTA

**P.S.:**
George saw the photo. "Michelle, this man has MORE bags on his table than I'VE ever bought you."

"George. You have never bought me a bag."

"...I'm fixing that on March 20th."

We'll see, George. We'll see.

📲 **Text AFR995E to 94923.** 🖤

**Why this works:**

- **PHOTO = tangible proof.** Not a claim. A picture. A desk. Eight bags.
- **Gift-buying = desire multiplier.** Reader thinks: who would I buy for? Mom? Sister? Best friend?
- **Previous sale → this sale.** "If the MLK sale made this man buy eight bags, what will March Madness do?"
- **Scale through STORY**, not marketing. One man. Eight women. That says more than "register now."
- **Reviews cascade naturally** — other gift-buyers confirm the pattern. This isn't one outlier.
