## 💡 Key Takeaways cho Future Campaigns

1. **6-email teaser sequence** trải đều 6 ngày trước sale — mỗi email reveal thêm 1 layer
2. **Character-driven storytelling** — mỗi email có nhân vật khác nhau, tạo "soap opera" effect
3. **SMS registration gate** — build private list + tạo commitment micro-action
4. **No Social Media stance** — biến limitation thành exclusivity advantage
5. **Math transparency** — show cụ thể donation math + product value math
6. **Progressive urgency** — từ soft tease → hard deadline, social proof tăng dần
7. **FAQ email cuối** — xử lý objections right before launch
8. **Dual-mission framing** — "Treat yourself AND support the community" — removes guilt from buying
