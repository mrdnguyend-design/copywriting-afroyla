### Skills to Know About
- `/daily-brief` — start of every workday; opens the current weekly plan and generates today's email + SMS brief.
- `/write-email` — run the 8-phase Infotainment email workflow (Michelle's voice, one story, one throughline).
- `/write-sms` — draft an SMS flash deal from the 10-angle rotation.
- `/finalize-email` — convert an approved email draft into Klaviyo-ready HTML.
- `/finalize-sms` — finalize an approved SMS for Postscript.
- `/idea-research` — harvest customer voices + develop READY items for the Story Bank.
- `/weekly-planning` — build the 7-day content calendar + revenue projection.
- `/monthly-strategy` — monthly strategy + P&L (used sparingly, start of month).
- `/review-craft` — Daniel Throssell–style proofread of a draft (most-used skill here).
- `/review-brand` — check a draft against Michelle's voice + Afroyla brand rules.
- `/review-strategy` — pressure-test an angle/offer before writing.
- `/simplify` — review changed code/content for reuse and cut bloat.
