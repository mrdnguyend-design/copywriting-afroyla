Why This Cause.

"This one is personal.

Type 2 diabetes runs in my family. The kind where you watch your mama count pills on a Sunday morning. The kind where your auntie skips dessert at Thanksgiving — and nobody says a word.

I've watched the strongest women I know fight this quietly. And I've watched the system look the other way.

Then I found CYL2 — the only diabetes prevention program in this country built from the ground up for Black women. Not adapted. Not translated. Created by Black women. For Black women. Recognized by the CDC.

When I started Afroyla, I told myself this brand would stand for something bigger than beautiful bags.

We're proving it."

— Michelle, Founder of Afroyla

What This Program Has Done

Enrolled nearly 5,000 women since 2014 and reached millions through awareness campaigns

Cut participants' risk of developing type 2 diabetes by up to 58%

Guided women to achieve 5–7% weight loss and maintain 150 minutes of weekly physical activity

Saves each participant an estimated $4,552 in medical costs — in just 2 years

2,200 women actively in the program today — online, accessible, free

Created the first culturally tailored curriculum recognized by the CDC — designed by and for Black women

This program works. And your purchase helps fund it.
