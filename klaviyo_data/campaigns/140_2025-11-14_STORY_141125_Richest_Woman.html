I am the richest man in the world.

No, seriously.

Not Bezos. Not Elon.

Me.

A Black woman with a laptop, a warehouse full of leather, and a stress snack drawer. 😂

After that email I sent the other day about copycats…
something wild happened.

My bank account did not explode.
(Okay. It wiggled a little. I bought extra snacks.)

But my inbox?

Filthy rich.

Queens from New York, DC, Miami, Texas, Cali, even Barbados…
all writing back.

Stories.
Confessions.
“Michelle, let me tell you what happened when I carried that bag…”

I sat there and read every single one like it was a love letter.

And I’m not joking…
I’m about to print them, put them in an album,
and show them off like baby pictures. 😭

“Here’s my first ‘you saved my confidence’ email…”
“Here’s the one where she wore the bag to chemo…”
“Here’s the one from Barbados that almost made me cry in my cereal…”

But there was one email…

That made me stop scrolling.

From a “girl” who just retired.

And when I read it, I honestly thought:

“Ma’am.
Can I be you when I grow up?”

Here’s what she wrote:

“Hi Michelle, I'm 65 years old, a mother of 2, grandmother of 8 and great grandmother of 4 and a Leo. I retired in February 2020 and since that time I've been traveling, New York City, DC-Maryland area, Cancun, MX and the Bahamas.

I was compelled to tell you of my Today Story:

I took a friend out to lunch for his birthday. I wore a leopard print outfit and carried my Afroyla bag with me. The compliments I received for my outfit were amazing.

But the stares and compliments I received on my Afroyla bag itself made me feel special, like royalty, like an African Queen!!!!

I own many expensive purses. But this purse, the one I carried today… it was like an ancestor's aura with me the whole time.

I LOOOOVE MY AFROYLA PURSE & MATCHING WALLET!!!! Thank you so much for having US in mind when you designed your purses, totes, crossbodies and wallets. I truly appreciate it!!!

Love,
Tawanna H”

Do you see why I’m screaming???

Traveling.
Healthy.
Leo energy.
Kids, grandkids, great-grandkids.
Leopard print fit.
And walking around like an AFRICAN QUEEN with her Afroyla bag on her arm.

(I left her photo at the end of the email...)

Meanwhile me:
“I just hope my back still works at 65 and I remember all my passwords.” 🥲

I’m jealous in the best way.

I love her big, happy family.
I love that she’s still out here traveling and dressing FLY.
And I love that line:

“It was like an ancestor’s aura with me the whole time.”

That one hit me in the chest.

Because that’s my grandmother.
That’s my mother.
That’s your grandmother.
Your mother.
Your auntie.

The women who poured into us…
and still shrug and say, “I’m fine, baby, I don’t need anything.”

Reading her email, I could see my own grandmother.
My mother.

The women who stayed up late,
worried, prayed, cooked, worked…
and still walked out the door smelling good and looking sharp.

They’re the reason Afroyla even exists.

And they’re the reason I designed a whole collection in their honor.

Bags that don’t scream “trend”…
but softly say:

“This woman has lived. This woman is loved.”

We call it our Mother Bag Collection.

If you’ve been planning a gift for your mother…
or your grandmother…
or the “Tawanna” of your family who does everything for everybody…

this is your little nudge from the universe.

Right now we’re running our Better Than Black Friday deal on this collection:

30% off every bag + a free matching wallet

So picture this:

She opens the box.
Her hands run over the leather.
She sees that Black woman artwork that actually looks like her.

And instead of saying, “You shouldn’t have…”
she whispers:

“You really saw me.”

At the end of this email, I’ve tucked in Tawanna’s photo in her leopard print and Afroyla bag.

I hope when you see it,
you think of your own “Tawanna.”

Wishing all our mothers and grandmothers good health…
A young, playful soul…
And a long, peaceful life full of compliments and cute bags.

With so much love,

Michelle
CEO, Afroyla.com
