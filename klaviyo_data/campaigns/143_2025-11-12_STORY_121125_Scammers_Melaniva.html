I felt a lump in my throat. My hands clenched. My body trembled.

I felt bad.

Very bad.

Because someone used our name to scam.

Afroyla.

MY Afroyla.

The name I built with pride. With sweat. With years of love for Black women.

Stolen. Copied. Turned into a Facebook ad funnel of lies.

At first, I didn’t understand what was happening.

But then… the emails started rolling in.

And I realized — something was very, very wrong.

“Hi, are you aware or in connection with Melaniva bags?? She/They have the same bags at similar prices. I bought one from them only to realize they may not have been who they say they are…”

“I have not received anything yet. -$90.80. MELANIVA MELANIVA TORONTO ON DBT PURCHASE ON 08/31…”

“Are you affiliated with Melaniva? I received a commercial on Facebook and it looks like your bags. Their site looks very professional.”

Let me be clear:

No.

That is NOT us.

There’s a scam company calling itself Melaniva... pretending to be Afroyla.

They copied our bag designs.

Copied our site structure.

Even tried to mimic our tone of voice.

But you know what they didn’t copy?

The part where we actually ship your order.

Sending. The. Actual. Bag.

If you’re lucky, you might get a low-quality knockoff in six months.

If you’re not?

Your bag is on a one-way cruise to Neverland.

And your $90? Sippin’ a piña colada in Shanghai right now.

Now, I’m not sharing this to scare you.

I’m sharing this to protect you.

Because I know what this bag means to you.

It’s not just an accessory.

It’s a moment. A statement. A reflection of who you are.

That feeling of unwrapping your bag and thinking, “This? This is me.”

I will NOT let that moment be stolen from you by a scammer in a basement with a Canva Pro account.

So here’s what I want you to do:

If you’re going to shop Afroyla — shop the real Afroyla.

We’re proudly Black-owned.

Our warehouse is in Arizona. We ship in 1–2 days. Most orders arrive within 4–6 days.

Not weeks. Not “maybe.” Not “if the stars align.”

Days.

And the quality? Over 500 glowing reviews on Trustpilot. Over 1,000 verified customer reviews on our website.

Real people. Real stories. Real pride.

(Because let’s be honest… our reviews say it better than I ever could.)

So if you’re ready to order a bag that actually arrives — and makes you feel everything you hoped for — here’s where you go:

👉 Click here to shop the real Afroyla

And if you already got scammed?

Report them. Call your bank. Screenshot the ad and send it to me.

Because this isn’t just about handbags anymore.

It’s about protecting the stories we carry.

The pride we wear.

And the joy we deserve.

We don’t play when it comes to Black excellence.

We protect it.

We rise together.

Michelle
Founder, Afroyla
