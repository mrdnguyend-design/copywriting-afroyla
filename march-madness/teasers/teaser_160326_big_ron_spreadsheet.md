One of the guys — everyone calls him Big Ron, don't ask — texted me last week..." | Cold reader doesn't know Big Ron from E4. | **3. Screenshot → Google Sheet** | "It was a Google Sheet. Four columns." | Daniel: screenshot was described but not SHOWN. A Google Sheet with four columns = reader SEEs it. |

| **5. "He does not."** | "He does not work better under pressure. But we're going to let him figure that out on his own." → "He does not." | Tighter. Two words. The period does the work. "Let him figure that out" was narrating more than showing. |
